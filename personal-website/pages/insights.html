The Metaverse and the Accounting Profession

This paper systematically explores the concept of the metaverse and highlights its significance for accounting research. It examines how consensus-based trust mechanisms and redefined materiality concepts enabled by microtransactions could inspire innovative accounting approaches. A 10-year research roadmap is proposed to guide future scholarly and practical advancements in accounting.
