I believe in fostering a learning environment that combines theoretical foundations with practical applications. My courses integrate data analytics tools and real-world case studies to prepare students for the modern accounting landscape.

I bring industry-aligned content to the classroom, drawing on my CPA Australia membership and prior industry experience at Gordon & Gotch Australia. My teaching philosophy emphasises critical thinking, cross-cultural awareness, and the ethical dimensions of accounting practice.
