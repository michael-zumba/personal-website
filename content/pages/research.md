My research primarily explores how emerging digital technologies are reshaping accounting practice and financial reporting. I am particularly interested in using textual analysis, machine learning, and big data methods to derive new insights into corporate disclosure, market behaviour, and regulatory frameworks.

My work has been published in leading accounting journals including *Meditari Accountancy Research* (ABDC-A), *Industrial Management & Data Systems* (ABDC-A), *International Review of Economics & Finance* (ABDC-A), and *Journal of International Accounting, Auditing and Taxation*, among others.

I welcome research collaborations. Feel free to reach out if you share similar interests.
