I have recently joined Auckland University of Technology as a Senior Lecturer (Above the Bar) in Accounting. This marks an exciting new chapter after five rewarding years at Lincoln University, where I had the privilege of serving as Acting Head of Department and supervising a growing group of talented PhD candidates.

## What This Means for My Research

My research agenda continues to focus on the intersection of emerging digital technologies and accounting practice. I am currently working on several projects:

- **Cybersecurity risk disclosure**: Using topic modelling to understand how firms communicate cyber risks
- **Disclosure complexity**: Examining how accounting standard mentions in NZX annual reports relate to market reactions
- **Metaverse and accounting**: Exploring how virtual environments may reshape the profession

## Teaching and Supervision

At AUT, I look forward to contributing to courses in financial accounting, auditing, and digital finance. I will continue supervising my PhD candidates on topics ranging from digital asset valuation to sustainability disclosures.
