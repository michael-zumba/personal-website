Welcome to my new personal website! I've rebuilt it from the ground up using **Next.js** and **Tailwind CSS**.

## Why the Change?

As an academic, I need a platform where I can easily share my research, teaching materials, and thoughts on the industry. While there are many great platforms out there, I wanted something that offered:

1. **Full Customization**: Ability to tweak every pixel.
2. **Markdown Support**: Writing in Markdown is essential for my workflow (especially with R and Python code).
3. **Performance**: Static site generation for blazing fast load times.
